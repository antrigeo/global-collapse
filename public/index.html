HOW TO PLAY

🌍 STORY

A deadly virus has collapsed civilization. Cities are overrun. You drop in as a survivor — fight NPCs and enemy players to be the last one standing.

🎯 GOAL

Survive. Kill to earn coins and score. Use coins at the shop to buy better weapons and vehicles. The safe zone shrinks — stay inside!

💰 COINS & SHOP

Kill NPCs = +5 coins. Kill players = +25 coins. Open the shop with the 🛒 button to buy weapons and vehicles. Vehicles spawn near you!

📱 MOBILE

🕹 Left joystick = Move | 🔴 FIRE = Shoot | 🟡 PICK UP = Loot/Enter vehicle | 💊 USE = Use item | 🛒 SHOP = Open shop

💻 PC CONTROLS

WASD = Move | Mouse = Aim | Click = Shoot | F/E = Pickup/Enter vehicle | 1 = Use item | B = Open shop

🔒 PRIVATE ROOMS

Create a room, share the 6-letter code. All friends enter the same code. If your group is smaller than 20, online players fill the rest automatically.
