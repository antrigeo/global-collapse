HOW TO PLAY

🌍 STORY

A deadly virus has spread worldwide. Cities are falling one by one. You are a survivor — choose a city, drop in, and fight to be the last one standing.

🎯 GOAL

Survive as long as possible. Kill infected NPCs and enemy players. The safe zone shrinks over time — stay inside or you'll take damage!

📱 MOBILE CONTROLS

🕹Left joystick = Move
🔴Red button = Shoot toward enemies
🟡Yellow button = Pick up loot
🔵Blue button = Use medkit/food

💻 PC CONTROLS

WASD = Move  |  Mouse = Aim
Click = Shoot  |  F = Pickup
1 = Eat food  |  2 = Use medkit
E = Enter/Exit vehicle

🍖 LOOT & SURVIVAL

Find food to restore hunger, medkits to heal HP, weapons to fight better, and armor to reduce damage.

🚗 VEHICLES

Find cars, boats, and planes on the map. Walk near them and press E (or yellow button) to enter. Vehicles are faster but make noise — other players can hear you!

⚔️ WEAPONS

Start with fists. Find Pistol → SMG → Rifle → Sniper. Better weapons need higher score to unlock. Kill NPCs and players to earn score!
